## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Yanshan University under the supervision of Prof. [Shunfu Jin](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-5845-5601), Prof. [Haoye Tian](https://haoyetiancoder.github.io/), and Prof. [Jiadong Ren](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/author/37087377087). I am also the CTO of Jisuanke Co., Ltd. I obtained a bachelor's and master's degree from Beihang University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhoujun Li. I am a Senior Member of the China Computer Federation (CCF) and an external supervisor at Wuhan University. Represented Beihang University in the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) World Finals and achieved 27th place.
